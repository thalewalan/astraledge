About Our Craft

Welcome to Astraledge by Shari

Astraledgebyshari is an event design and management company dedicated to creating personalized and thoughtfully curated event experiences. Each project is approached with a focus on individuality, ensuring that every detail reflects the client’s vision, personality, and purpose.

We provide end-to-end event services including concept development, thematic design, stage and spatial styling, and complete event coordination. Our work is driven by creativity, detail, and meaningful design, transforming ideas into visually elegant and memorable experiences.

Our portfolio includes corporate functions, product launches, awards ceremonies, graduations, housing project unveilings, and private celebrations.

We are proud to have worked with leading brands and organizations including Multilac, Macktiles, Prime Lands, Institute of Information Technology, Dankotuwa, Spar Supermarkets, Lions Club International events, Sunshine Holdings Pvt Ltd, and Mega Lifesciences Pvt Ltd.
